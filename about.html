A Few Words About Us

Bhadra Agencies was established in the year 2005 in Bangalore, with the prime objective of catering the customer needs through the distribution of unique and Quality products. We primarily focus into Poultry Feed Additives and health Products which is driven by the customer relationship with value added services to our esteemed customers. Further we have established in the year 2009 atNamakkalwhich is the major commercial Layer belt to cover the entire Tamil Nadu and Kerela.

The company brings very strong fundamentals to the Poultry business, with the association of few technical experts who have extensively travelled and worked more than a decade in poultry sectorwhich is the boon to identify the ideal products which meets the customer need which leads to satisfaction. Since we are also ideally located in Tamil Nadu which is well connected to the Poultry pockets like Namakkal (EGG BASKET of India),Palladam (Breeder Hub)and the nearby state Kerela which is the major consumption centre,we are open with all the future establishments in any part of the poultry pockets in Tamil Nadu.

With this humble beginning, today the firm is marching towardstheanual turn over 8 million US $ and we are now associated with the leading MNCs operating in India which deals with Amino acids, and some unique products.

For the better services to our customers, our company is well equipped with the sufficient man power and the logistics arrangement. We have the sufficient number of vehicles so that the products can be delivered at the earliest locally and well connected with the transporters for the out station customers.
